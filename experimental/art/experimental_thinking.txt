ecosystem simulator meets snappy arcade game
how should this game feel to play?
    game should be minimalist and snappy, easy to control and pick up
    the game will have lots of simple parts which are easy to know when beginning but these parts will come together to make much deeper gameplay when the player becomes more adept at the game
each loop will last from 2:30 to 8:00 minutes where when the timer elapses some disaster occurs
    the player must make it back to their nest successfully to hibernate through the disaster

**every creature functions based on these main parameters**
    hunger
        a rate, value, and max value.
        it is recommended to determine behavior based on a ratio: value/max.
        controls the creature's urge to seek and consume food.
        this rate is directly proportional to the velocity of the creature
    nutrition
        a base amount of hunger satiated when the creature is consumed by another.
    fat
        acts as long term storage of nutrition to determine certain characteristics.
        fat is gained when the creature eats while not hungry.
        fat is lost when the creature has a high hunger percentage.
    age
        controls the life stage of the creature.
        higher age means faster hunger rate.

how should i implement a system for creatures to track each other and determine their relationships? the relationship status between creatures should determine their state and therefore behavior. also determine if this design is too involved or if the scope is too wide.
    so creature A will track every creature in its sightline PLUS the creatures in its memory (albiet less precisely). say creature A spots creature B. creature A will create a reference in its mind of creature B and determine its relationship. depending on certain factors like distance, its favorite foods, its current emotional state, its mass, etc, creature A will choose a relationship type to associate with creature B (know that this relationship type can switch and change over time). relationship strength should be measured by a ratio with 0 being no relationship and 1 being a highly strong relationship.
