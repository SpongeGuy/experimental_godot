# creature pack 1:

**plopp**
- creates edible orbs
- wanders around aimlessly
- dances occasionally, spitting more orbs

**moo**
- wanders around aimlessly
- eats orbs
- flees from threats

**imp**
- wanders around
- likes to be near walls
- attacks nearby creatures

**floroid**
- plantlike creature
- dies in one hit
- digs underground unseen
- comes up occasionally to drop a fruit of its type
- damages walls it digs under

**stone**
- hard as a rock
- moves slowly
- wanders around
- collides with creatures like terrain
- likes to be near walls
- likes to be near other stones

**salmon**
- flops around aimlessly
- shoots pellets at nearby creatures
- likes being in water

**rud**
- hops strategically
- eats salmon
- causes shockwaves when it hops

**bitwit**
- friendly puffball
- is knocked around easily
- follows creatures around

**hoary**
- designates an area as 'his lawn'
- attacks any creature on his lawn
- likes the **gnome**

**grue**
- comes out only at night
- ruthlessly hunts down other creatures

# creature pack 2:

**tarantorra**
- creates a home web
- guards its web until hungry
- stalks and pounces on prey and webs them up
- brings prey back to its home

**carpo**
- eats a fruit then becomes a big version of it
- when hit it creates more fruits

**ceefor**
- sits in an area until damaged
- when harmed, it runs around panicked
- after a while it detonates in a large explosion

**runbone**
- dashes quickly across the screen
- stuns nearby creatures
- damages creatures it runs over

**spatuler**
- drills through walls
- searches for valuables and treasure
- likes **stone** creatures and wants to breed them

**kori**
- moves quickly around
- hangs around its nest
- quickly grabs food and brings them back to its nest
- feeds its nested children
- attacks nearby creatures

**hog**
- travels in packs with other hogs
- follows the most dominant hog
- prefers hunting *large* creatures

**giant worm** (*large*)
- multiple body segments which trail behind its head
- eats creatures in its path
- digs through walls easily

**mooer** (*large*)
- a massive cow which formed from too many cows being in one area
- wanders around aimlessly
- eats grass (and sometimes creatures whole)
- flees from threats
- regularly produces cheese

**rage beast** (*large*)
- is dormant until too many creatures are nearby
- goes into a rampage until it finds a quiet area
- attacks indiscriminately

# creature pack 3:

**borega**
- a colorful moth creature
- flies overhead, charming creatures
- leads charmed creatures into one area

**totango**
- wields percussion instruments
- when enough creatures are nearby, starts a conga line
- leads the parade until it is damaged

**starman**
- likes to be near trees
- creates smaller versions of itself (they stun nearby creatures)
- floats around aimlessly
- does contact damage

**tecton**
- jumps long distances over terrain
- jumps on top of creatures to catch them and eat them

**waddler**
- wanders around with its children
- if the parent is killed, the children become ravenous

**anhurgir**
- demonic spirit that travels through walls
- spits fire at creatures it doesn't like

**wind wam**
- creates directional gusts of wind
- if the gust is powerful enough, it stuns the creature
- spreads fire if there is any nearby

**wind lord**
- casts spells on **wind wams** to make their gusts more powerful
- summons dangerous cyclones, causing entities to smash into each other

**nutralon**
- summons fruit
- casts spells on fruit to make them bigger and more nutritive
- wanders around aimlessly

**mankey**
- friends with other mankeys
- throws stones at creatures it does not like
- wants to be around other mankeys
- picks up and roots **palmoids** in the ground, preventing them from moving

**slumba**
- likes treasure
- picks a spot to designate its home
- scampers around and brings treasure back to its home
- attacks nearby creatures
- mauls creatures near its trove

# creature pack 4:


**cat**
- naps in corners
- gets agitated if awoken suddenly
- scratches and runs away rapidly

**blaze**
- has orbiting flame pillars
- shoots its pillars at creatures
- catches the environment on fire

**pyre**
- continuously on fire
- moves quickly but pauses often
- rarely shoots a wave of flame

**bongo** (*large*)
- beats on its drum, creating shockwaves
- gets angry when hungry, drumming faster
- shockwaves spread fire if there is any nearby

**frozer**
- causes nearby tiles to freeze, making them slippery
- throws snowballs at creatures
- if cold enough, freezes creatures and items solid

**bumble**
- stomps around, damaging those near it
- exhales a blizzard, freezing the area in front of it
- breathes on **frozers**, powering them up and making their cold colder

**slushy**
- ice tornado spirit
- shoots ice shards rapidly and plants them in walls
- makes terrain brittle

**gordon** (*large*)
- slowly rolls around
- when nearby a creature it attempts to grab it
- grabbed creatures are then swallowed
- if **gordon** is killed, all swallowed creatures burst out

**slick**
- rides around on its board
- spreads oil on the ground which can stick itself to creatures
- makes fire do more damage and last longer
- attacks nearby creatures

**kernel**
- waddles around
- if lit on fire, it will burst into several popped corns
- popped corns will jump and run frantically around
- popped corns attack nearby enemies
- popped corns eventually burst, causing a small explosion

# creature pack 5

**puzzle**
- hard as a rock
- latches onto creatures or walls to form temporary structures

**imp**
- wanders around aimlessly
- projects fake versions of itself
- attacks nearby creatures

**dungeon cube**
- only travels in four cardinal directions
- charges up energy, then slides until it hits a wall
- destroys anything in its path

**alchemite**
- transforms items into any random object
- wanders around aimlessly

**mimic**
- curious of other creatures
- follows them around and copies their abilities

**grub**
- travels slowly
- creates sludge on the ground that makes creatures slow down

**gloob**
- hops one tile at a time
- creates lightweight breakable terrain when it hops

**poltergeist**
- shuffles terrain around itself (including all entities on these tiles)
- floats around, passing through hard terrain
- likes being near other creatures

**clone**
- wanders around aimlessly
- attacks nearby creatures
- clones itself when harmed, halving its current health

**cultist**
- shoots explosive fireballs at a target
- fireballs can travel over terrain
- does not move, teleports nearby other creatures

**kisse**
- slurps up nearby projectiles and spits them back to their origin
- likes being in open spaces or in water

# creature pack 6

**bounce blob**
- a round bouncy slime creature
- likes being around walls
- reacts dramatically to other creatures bumping into it
- bounces off of walls
- grows larger with every bounce until it bursts into smaller blobs

**palmoid**
- plantlike creature
- throws coconuts at nearby creatures
- coconuts are fruits and can be eaten
- moves very slowly

**fernoid**
- plantlike creature
- likes to be near other **fernoids**
- creatures next to fernoids are invisible and undetectable
- regularly drops edible leaves

**spectroid**
- can creep through terrain
- occasionally drops edible spectral flowers
- spectral flowers allow creatures to pass through walls

**tarrite**
- grows crystals which reflect projectiles
- crystals also react chaotically to contact damage
- plants crystals in the ground occasionally

**spike ball**
- rolls around causing massive contact damage
- damage is higher the faster it goes
- its velocity can only be increased
- if it runs into a wall at a specific velocity it shatters

**blossom**
- tangles creatures up in vines
- if creatures are tangled up for long enough, they are dragged into the soil
- this creates a random plantlike creature

**bomba**
- absorbs energy from fire
- when damaged, it explodes using its built up energy
- can build up energy infinitely

**gnome**
- wants to be around plantlike creatures
- waters plantlike creatures for a chance to duplicate them

**puff wizard**
- gets upset when creatures are harmed
- runs near just harmed creatures
- grows mushrooms which expel healing spores

# creature pack 7

**architect**
- creates lines of walls nearby other creatures

**popbug**
- infests walls
- slowly creates more **popbugs** which infest adjacent walls
- pop out of walls when destroyed
- attack nearby creatures
- can infest the **stone**

**door master**
- creates intradimensional doors which lead to other areas

**keyman**
- sometimes pops out of doors when used
- runs away, creating treasure
- attacks nearby creatures

**scavenger**
- a more complex being
- has a designated home belonging to other scavengers
- go out in parties hunting or looking for treasure
- can be traded with for their exotic crafts

**warden** (*large*)
- can alter reality to get to where it wants
- changes tiles to dramatically different things
- hunts **door masters**
- hostile to creatures that provoke it

**bubbler**
- wanders around aimlessly
- blows bubbles which can catch creatures
- bubbles are highly affected by wind
- bubbles transport creatures over terrain, but eventually pop

**spin wheel**
- like the **dungeon cube**, but only travels in diagonals

**plant wizard**
- casts spells on creatures to turn them plantlike
- wanders around aimlessly
- roots creatures occasionally
